Discover the practice of 4 makers you should be looking out for this month.
Curated by Ana Bruque.

CLAUDIA WASSICZEK

London, UK

Rough, sharp and surprising textures put together to create organic ceramic sculptures and collage pieces. Claudia predominately makes her clay body from scratch (mixing it up from raw-dry materials).

#ceramic #collage #scratch

Website

LISE HERUD BRATEN

London, UK

Organic and iregular forms Skew and distort from their initial geometry and perfect symmetry, Barbara's organic and iregular forms explores the multi-layered effects of nature by employing grogged porcelain and Nerikomi techniques.

#porcelain #nerikomi #smoke-fired

VERITY HOWARD

London, UK

Contemplative forms, that capture feelings, moods, atmospheres and a sense of place. Verity, responds to history and places by creating slab built ceramic works and using clay as a medium for drawing and monoprinting.

#slab-built #drawing #monoprinting
