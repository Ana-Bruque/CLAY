CLAY is a platform dedicated to pottery & ceramics.

CLAY is all about potting, potters and pottery!

Dedicated to pottery and ceramics, CLAY is a platform to discover studios to learn pottery across London and get inspired by finding talented makers across the UK through our monthly Makers to Watch and our curated selection of galleries across London.
